Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SnapLead, operated by Jovilex Premium Services Limited under Komir Holdings, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data. SnapLead serves businesses and their customers across multiple jurisdictions, including the United States, United Kingdom, European Union, Canada, and other regions.

1. Who we are

SnapLead is an AI-powered lead response service operated by Jovilex Premium Services Limited, a company under Komir Holdings. For the purposes of data protection law, Jovilex Premium Services Limited is the data processor. The business using SnapLead (the account holder) is the data controller for their customers' data.

2. Data we collect

From business owners (account holders)

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business name (provided at signup)
  • Industry and business description
  • Website URL (optional)
  • Account credentials (password stored as a salted hash — we never store plain text passwords)
  • IP address at signup (for fraud prevention)
  • Payment information (processed by our payment provider — we do not store card details)

From customers (people who submit enquiries)

  • Name, email address, and phone number (provided on the lead form)
  • Responses to qualification questions configured by the business
  • IP address (for rate limiting and fraud prevention)

Automatically collected

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, response times
  • Device and browser information via standard web protocols

3. How we use data

  • To provide the SnapLead service: generating AI responses, delivering emails, displaying leads in the dashboard
  • To communicate with account holders about their account, billing, and service updates
  • To prevent fraud and abuse: duplicate account detection, rate limiting, disposable email blocking
  • To improve the service: aggregate analytics on response times, usage patterns, and system performance
  • To attribute referrals: tracking which partner referred which business for commission purposes

4. AI processing

SnapLead uses artificial intelligence (provided by Anthropic's Claude API) to generate personalised responses to customer enquiries. Customer data submitted through the lead form is sent to the AI service for the sole purpose of generating a response. The AI does not retain customer data after generating the response. The AI is instructed to never provide professional advice, quote prices, or make commitments.

5. Data sharing

We share personal data only with the following categories of service providers, and only to the extent necessary to deliver the service:

  • Anthropic (AI response generation)
  • Resend (email delivery)
  • Supabase (database hosting)
  • Vercel (application hosting)
  • Payment processor (subscription billing — when applicable)

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with advertisers. We do not use personal data for purposes unrelated to providing the SnapLead service.

6. Data retention

  • Active accounts: data is retained for the duration of the account
  • Cancelled accounts: data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted
  • Expired trials: data is retained for 30 days after trial expiry, then permanently deleted
  • Rate limiting logs: deleted after 24 hours
  • We may retain aggregated, anonymised data indefinitely for analytics purposes

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to data portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: object to certain processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at noreply@jovilex.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. GDPR compliance (UK and EU users)

For users in the United Kingdom and European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing personal data is:

  • Contract performance: processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interest: fraud prevention, service improvement, and business analytics
  • Consent: where required by law, such as email communications beyond service delivery

Data may be transferred outside the UK/EEA to service providers in the United States. Such transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses and the service providers' own data protection commitments.

9. CCPA compliance (California users)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information. You may request disclosure of the categories of personal information collected, the purposes of collection, and the categories of third parties with whom it is shared. Contact us at noreply@jovilex.com to exercise these rights.

10. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Password hashing using PBKDF2 with SHA-512 (100,000 iterations)
  • JWT authentication with HMAC-SHA256 signing
  • Row-level security on database tables
  • Input sanitisation to prevent injection attacks

No system is completely secure. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Cookies

SnapLead uses minimal cookies and local storage for authentication purposes (storing your login session). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. No cookie consent banner is required as we only use strictly necessary cookies.

12. Children

SnapLead is a business service not intended for use by individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered account holders. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

14. Contact

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, contact us at noreply@jovilex.com or visit jovilex.com.
