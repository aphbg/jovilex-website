1. Scope of Work

Jovilex agrees to deliver the services and deliverables described above within the stated timeline. Any work outside this scope will be discussed and agreed in writing before being undertaken, and may require an additional fee.

2. Client Responsibilities

The client agrees to provide all materials, access, and approvals required for delivery in a timely manner. Delays in providing required assets may extend the delivery timeline proportionally.

3. Revisions

One round of revisions is included in the fee. Additional revision rounds will be quoted separately. A revision is defined as changes to the agreed deliverables — not changes to the agreed scope.

4. Payment Terms

50% of the total fee is due before work begins. The remaining 50% is due on delivery of the final deliverables. For retainer engagements, the full monthly fee is due at the start of each month. No work begins until payment is confirmed.

5. Intellectual Property

Upon receipt of full payment, all deliverables created specifically for this engagement are transferred to the client. Jovilex retains the right to showcase the work in its portfolio unless a confidentiality agreement states otherwise.

6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep all project-related information confidential. If a separate NDA has been signed, its terms take precedence over this clause.

7. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with seven (7) days written notice. If terminated by the client, fees for work already completed are non-refundable. If terminated by Jovilex, all completed work to date will be delivered to the client.
