Questions from attorneys and firm administrators

Does the AI provide legal advice to potential clients?

Absolutely not. The AI is explicitly instructed to never assess a case, speculate on outcomes, or provide legal guidance of any kind. It acknowledges the enquiry, affirms that the firm handles the relevant practice area, confirms confidentiality, and informs the potential client that an attorney will follow up. Every response stays within ethical boundaries.

Is the communication confidential?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. The AI response and lead data are stored in a secure, access-controlled database. Only authorised users on your firm's account can view enquiries. The form itself communicates to the potential client that their submission is treated as confidential.

How does this handle urgent matters?

If a potential client selects "Urgent — need help immediately," the lead is flagged with an urgent badge in your dashboard. The notification email to your firm includes "URGENT" in the subject line. The AI response emphasises that the team is being notified right away. You see it the moment it arrives.

Can we customise the intake questions?

Yes. The form comes pre-configured with five legal-intake questions: practice area, urgency, situation description, consultation preference, and referral source. You can modify, add, or remove questions through your dashboard at any time to match your firm's intake process.

Does this integrate with our case management system?

Direct CMS integration (Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther) is available on the Scale plan. On Starter and Growth, you receive email notifications and manage all enquiries through a web-based dashboard. Calendar integration for booking consultations is available on Growth.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

Your account pauses. No charges are applied. If your firm wishes to continue, select a plan. If not, your data remains accessible for 30 days. No contracts. Cancel at any time.
