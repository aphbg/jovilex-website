Questions from contractors and service pros

Does the AI quote prices to customers?

Never. The AI acknowledges what the customer described, reassures them that your company handles this type of work regularly, and tells them someone will be in touch to schedule an assessment. It does not quote prices, diagnose issues, or make commitments on your behalf. Every response stays professional and within the boundaries you'd expect from your best office staff.

What happens with emergency requests?

If a customer selects "Emergency — need someone today," the lead is flagged with a red urgent badge. Your notification email has "URGENT" in the subject line. The AI response to the customer emphasises that the team is being alerted immediately. You see it the second it comes in.

I'm a one-man operation. Is this worth it?

It's built for you. You're the one who can't answer the phone while you're on a ladder. You're the one losing jobs because you call back 3 hours later. SnapLead is your front office when you don't have one. One extra job per month and it's paid for itself many times over.

Can I customise the questions customers see?

Yes. When you sign up, five home-services-specific questions are pre-loaded: service type, urgency, description of the issue, property type, and preferred scheduling time. You can edit, add, or remove any of them from your dashboard.

How do I put this on my website?

You get a standalone page link immediately that you can share anywhere — your website, Google Business Profile, Yelp, Nextdoor, Facebook, truck wrap, business cards. An embeddable widget that sits directly on your existing website is coming soon.

What happens after the free trial?

Your account pauses. No charges, no surprise bills. If you want to continue, pick a plan. If not, your data stays for 30 days. No contracts, cancel anytime. You can restart whenever you want.
