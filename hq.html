Each building serves a distinct function. All are interconnected by walkways, green corridors, and shared infrastructure. The campus operates 24 hours.

Komir Holdings
The Executive Building
Komir Holdings executive office. The nerve centre of a multi-venture holding company. Board room, founder's office, investor meeting spaces, and the legal and financial operations that govern every venture in the portfolio.
Jovilex · Kinetic · SnapLead
The Operations Building
The core team workspace for Jovilex (AI-powered global services), Kinetic (the world's first intellectual identity platform — nothing comparable exists globally), and SnapLead (AI lead response SaaS, live and in market across four verticals). Where products are built, clients are served, and software ships daily. Open-plan and private offices, build rooms, and a dedicated demo space for client presentations.
Forex · Wealth Engine · Academy
The Trading Floor
Multi-screen trading desks. The capital engine of Komir Holdings. Active forex operations across multiple prop firm accounts with an FTMO world record attempt in preparation. The trading floor also operates as Nigeria's first physical proprietary capital management firm — managing external capital for high-net-worth individuals, state governments, and community development funds, with structured investment plans and phased returns. Adjacent to the trading floor: the Komir Forex Academy — an Ivy League-standard trading school offering six-month and twelve-month diploma programmes at executive level. Graduates are positioned as chartered forex traders managing institutional-grade portfolios. Nothing at this standard exists on the continent.
Command Centre · Payvif
The GovTech Wing
Secure operations centre for Command Centre (intelligence and operational efficiency platform) and Payvif (government revenue aggregation). Palantir-model positioning — Command Centre enters as operational efficiency, Payvif follows as revenue digitisation. Prior ministerial invitation from the DRC government already received. One location only. Controlled access. Built to defence-grade security standards.
Praxis · Education
The School
An education industry disruptor. Nigerian premium families spend hundreds of billions of naira annually on schools that produce certificates but not capability. Praxis compresses world-class academics into two hours using AI and builds the life skills conventional schools ignore. Dedicated building with its own entrance, outdoor space, and child-specific security. Integration model available for existing schools alongside exclusive Praxis campuses — any school can adopt the system without competing. Parents drop their child here, walk to the operations building, and go to work.
Public-Facing
The Hub
Premium coworking space open to the public. Private booths for deep work and calls. Meeting rooms available for hire. A commercial front that generates revenue, builds community, and positions Komir as a physical presence in Abuja's business landscape. Open 24 hours.
Performance Infrastructure
The Wellness Centre
Fitness facility with strength and movement equipment. Recovery wing with sauna, rest pods, and decompression spaces. On-site nursery for children from 36 months with dedicated nannies. This building exists because performance requires recovery, and parents should never have to choose between their work and their child.
Founder · Private
The Founder's Office
A separate, private workspace — inaccessible to the public and disconnected from daily operations. This is where high-level strategy, creation, and deep work happen. The founder operates here three days a week. Every return to the campus brings new products, new milestones, and new records. The space where the next decade is designed.
Elruta · Nutrition Brand
The Kitchen
Not a canteen — a nutrition system. Everyone on campus eats by design: macro-calculated, protein-tracked meals engineered for sustained output. The menu spans lean proteins, performance bowls, premium Greek yogurt, L. reuteri probiotics yogurt, house-made protein shakes, clean weight gainers, and signature sauces — all developed to taste exceptional while hitting precise nutritional targets. Connected to the Wellness Centre: what you eat is designed alongside how you move and how you recover. Elruta operates as a commercial brand, serving the campus internally and distributing to corporate offices and the public. A blue ocean entry into Nigeria's performance nutrition market — no comparable brand currently serves the corporate wellness segment at this standard. Every product on the menu has been personally developed, tested, and validated — the Greek yogurt alone has attracted unsolicited investment interest.

Sound stage, editing suites, podcast booths, and a content production floor. Every piece of Komir content — from social media to broadcast television — originates here.

Operating Principles
24-hour functionality
The campus never closes. Different zones serve different hours. The trading floor follows global markets. The hub serves the public around the clock. The school operates daytime. The wellness centre is always available.
Recovery is infrastructure
Sleep, movement, decompression, and access to nature are not benefits. They are operational requirements engineered into every building and every walkway on the campus.
Parents don't choose
Childcare and education are on campus. No team member chooses between their work and their family. The child learns at Praxis. The parent builds at Jovilex. They are 200 metres apart.
Green is structural
Plants, herbs, grass, and natural light are not decoration. They are design requirements in every building and every outdoor space. People put their feet on grass during their workday.
One standard, applied globally
What is built in Abuja defines the DNA for every Komir workspace worldwide. This campus is the reference point. Every future location meets this standard or does not carry the name.
