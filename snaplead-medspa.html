Questions from clinic owners and aesthetic directors

Does the AI make medical claims or recommend treatments?

Never. The AI acknowledges the client's interest, expresses enthusiasm about the treatment category, and reassures them that a specialist will discuss their specific goals in detail. It does not recommend procedures, make medical claims, or quote pricing. Every response reflects the warm, professional tone of a luxury aesthetic practice.

Can the response tone match our brand?

Yes. You can choose between three tone settings — warm, professional, or direct — and the AI adapts accordingly. The responses are designed to sound like the best version of your front-of-house team: sophisticated, welcoming, and knowledgeable without overstepping.

We get a lot of Instagram DM enquiries. Does this help with those?

SnapLead currently works through your lead form page, which you can link directly from your Instagram bio, stories, or DM auto-replies. When someone DMs asking about treatments, your auto-reply directs them to the form. They fill it out, the AI responds instantly, and you get the brief. It turns unstructured DMs into qualified, actionable leads.

What questions do clients see on the form?

Five aesthetic-specific questions come pre-loaded: treatment interest, previous experience, goals, preferred timeline, and budget comfort. You can edit, add, or remove any of these from your dashboard to match your consultation process.

Can clients book a consultation directly?

On the Growth plan, you can connect your calendar (Calendly, Google Calendar, or booking link) so the AI response includes a direct booking option. On Starter, the response tells the client that someone will reach out to schedule — and you follow up from the dashboard.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

Your account pauses gracefully. No charges, no disruption. If you'd like to continue, select a plan. If not, your data remains available for 30 days. No contracts, cancel anytime.
