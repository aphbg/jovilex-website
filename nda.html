This agreement protects the confidential information shared by both parties during and after the engagement. Both parties agree to the following terms:

1. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential information includes all business, technical, financial, and operational information disclosed by either party during the engagement — whether written, verbal, or digital. This includes but is not limited to: business plans, strategies, pricing, customer data, source code, designs, trade secrets, internal documents, and any information marked or reasonably understood to be confidential.

2. Obligations

Both parties agree to: keep all confidential information strictly private; not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent; use it only for the purposes of the agreed engagement; and protect it with the same care used for their own confidential information.

3. Exclusions

This agreement does not apply to information that: was publicly available before disclosure; becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party; was independently developed without reference to confidential information; or is required to be disclosed by law.

4. Duration

This agreement remains in effect for the duration of the engagement and for two (2) years after its conclusion. Obligations regarding trade secrets continue indefinitely.

5. Return of Materials

Upon termination of the engagement, each party will return or destroy all confidential materials belonging to the other party upon request.

6. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
